About Us

Welcome to Tim's Trailer Time, where movies meet buttery popcorn dreams.

The Popcorn Beginnings

Once upon a time, in a humble red-and-white striped popcorn house, a young kernel named Tim was born. Tim wasn't like other kernels who were happy to pop for a Saturday matinee—Tim had dreams bigger than his cozy popcorn bucket. His home, adorned with homemade awards like "Best Solo Pop," was a testament to his unstoppable spirit.

Cinemas and Starry Nights

Tim would sneak into theaters, bouncing past security to sit beneath the projector’s glow, entranced by the magic of cinema. It was in those moments he realized he was destined to be not just a background snack but the star of the show.

The Journey to Stardom

Tim’s big break came when he donned his iconic black trench coat and shades, landing his first role as "The Cool Popcorn." Soon, he was on Hollywood posters, starring in hits like Pop Hard: Corn Chronicles and Kernel of Truth. Tim's smile lit up screens, and his catchphrase, “Life’s better buttered,” became legendary.

The Birth of Tim's Trailer Time

After years of stardom, Tim launched Tim’s Trailer Time, the go-to site for trailer lovers everywhere. Packed with thrills, laughs, and Tim’s own witty reviews, his site captures the magic of cinema in every clip, preview, and popcorn-worthy moment.

The Heart of the Magic

For Tim, cinema isn’t just about the movie—it’s about sharing the experience. At Tim’s Trailer Time, he brings that joy to every visitor, making sure each click is packed with excitement and a kernel of buttery humor.

Today, Tim’s not just a movie star. He’s a popcorn legend, the king of trailers, and your guide to the big screen. So, grab some popcorn and dive into the movie magic that awaits because, as Tim always says:

“Life’s better buttered - Tim”
